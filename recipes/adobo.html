Adobong Manok

Description

Chicken Adobo or Adobong Manok is a popular Filipino dish but it originated from the Spanish word adobar or marinate. By virtue of its etymology, it is a dish that involves marinating the chicken meat in soy sauce and some spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving parts
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3-4 pcs dried laurel leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorn
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken with the soy sauce and garlic then mix well.
  2. Marinate the chicken for about 1 hour, better if you could go for 3 hours.
  3. Heat the cooking oil in a pot.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade (reserve marinade) and pan-fry it when the oil gets hot enough.
  5. Cook each side of the chicken for about 2 minutes.
  6. Pour in the marinade including the garlic.
  7. Add-in the water and bring to a boil.
  8. Add-in the bay leaves and peppercorn.
  9. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender.
  10. Add-in the vinegar but do not stir. Cook for 10 minutes.
  11. Add-in the sugar and some salt to taste. Stir then remove from heat.
  12. Transfer to a serving plate, enjoy!
